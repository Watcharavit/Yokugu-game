Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Yokugu?

Yokugu is a word scambling game inspired by Hangman.

What are the rules?

- You have 100 health point, decrease 3 for each wrong answer

- If health point hits 0, you will die.

- If someone finishes the game, there will be 60 second left before the game end.

- Score will calculate from level and hp accordingly.

Why picking some word takes so long?

TL;DR : That word is not yet in our database.

Whenever a user validate a new word, it gets stored to our database. So the next time we search for this word, we can just look up in our database, not calling an API.

Why image and its definition are not related?

Sometimes words have more than one meaning. Ex. "Iron" = an actual iron or gold.

In the future we might use AI to match the definitions with the words, if time and money permits : ).
