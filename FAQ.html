Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Yokugu game?

Yokugu is a word scambling game inspired by Hangman.

What are the rules?

You have 100 health point, decrease 3 for each wrong answer
If health point hits 0, you will die.
If someone finishes the game, there will be 60 second left before the game end.
Score will calculate from level and hp accordingly.
